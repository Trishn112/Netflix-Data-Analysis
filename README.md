# Netflix-Data-Analysis
Data Analysis of Netflix Movies &amp; TV Shows using Python 

 ALL RESULT IN PONITS AFTER THE WHOLE ANALYSIS :-

->The following significant findings were attained following data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and visualization on  the Netflix dataset:

->Movies make up a far greater percentage of Netflix's content library than TV series do.

->After 2015, the platform's content addition rate skyrocketed, indicating aggressive expansion and worldwide growth.

->Netflix's emphasis on both Western and emerging markets is demonstrated by the fact that the United States contributes the most content, followed by India and the United Kingdom.

->The most popular genres are dramas and foreign films, demonstrating Netflix's focus on content that appeals to a wide audience and is emotionally compelling.

->It appears that Netflix primarily caters to adult viewers because the majority of its content is categorized under mature audience ratings like TV-MA and TV-14.


ALL CONCLUSION IN PONITS AFTER THE WHOLE ANALYSIS :-

Using Python, this Netflix Data Analysis project effectively investigated audience targeting, country-specific production, genre distribution, and content trends.
According to the analysis, Netflix's strategy is centered on mature audience engagement, global expansion, and movie-heavy content.

Netflix's quick rise to prominence as a top streaming service is demonstrated by the growing number of titles it added after 2015. Furthermore, Netflix's global content strategy is highlighted by the significant contributions from nations like the US and India.

In summary, this project shows how data analysis and visualization methods can be applied to real-world datasets to glean valuable insights that enhance comprehension of content strategies and business trends.
